## AI product decision system

Analytics tools show where people drop off, but not why. Session replays add context, but they are hard to scale. Without clear qualitative evidence at scale, teams debate priorities based on opinions rather than user reality.

TAMsense solves this by turning user behavior data into decision-ready product recommendations.

- Chat with your product to get quic answers based on entare database 
- Get qualitative evidence at scale so all the data is explained

## How it works

### Align on your positioning
TAMsense aligns with you on your product positioning and helps create clear artifacts for market segmentation, ideal customer profile, and value proposition.

### Analyze behavior
Our engine analyzes session data, clicks, and user paths to identify where customers struggle or fail to reach value.

### Get ready-to-go decisions
TAMsense gives specific, prioritized recommendations on what to do next to fix conversion leaks and guide users to value.
